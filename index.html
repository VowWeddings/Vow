Our Story

vow. began with our founder's own overwhelming wedding planning experience and a simple mission: to handle every stressful detail so you can focus on the joy. We believe every couple deserves a day that perfectly reflects their love story, and our passion is making that journey as beautiful as the destination.

Vision

To become the leading digital wedding-planning marketplace, a platform where couples design, plan and celebrate their dream weddings with ease and creativity

Mission

To empower couples to bring their unique vision to life through a seamless digital experience that blends inspiration, organization, and human expertise — taking away all the noise so they can focus on what truly matters: their story.
